Education

A First Semester Senior at the University of Southern Indiana, majoring in Computer Science, and minoring in Photography.

While she originally went to University of Evansville, majoring in Electrical Engineering, she transfered to University of Southern Indiana in the fall of 2021, taking the chance to also change her major after finding that she enjoyed the topic much more while helping out with Girls Who Code and taking a required class at the University of Evansville.

When transfering she found that most of her general education classes had been completed already so under advice decided to take the chance to round out her education somewhat by minoring in Photograpy which increased her creative skills.
